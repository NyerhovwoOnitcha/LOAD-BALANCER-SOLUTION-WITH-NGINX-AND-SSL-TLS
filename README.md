# LOAD-BALANCER-SOLUTION-WITH-NGINX-AND-SSL-TLS
# This project gives
- A good understanding of the concepts of digital certificates and security technologies (SSL and TSL) for protection against Man in the middle attacks during transfer of sensitive data between clients and webservers. 
You will create a digital certificate for your website and migrate your website from http to https as well as creating a cronjob to renew your certificate

- A good understanding of Domain registrars, hosting providers, name servers, DNS as well as the types of DNS records. 
You will attach an elastic ip to your nginx Load balancer and associate your domain to the elastic IP
